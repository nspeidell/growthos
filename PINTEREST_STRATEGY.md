# Reunion Pinterest Strategy

## Core Insight

Pinterest is NOT a social platform. It is a **visual discovery engine + emotional search engine**.

People use it to:
- Imagine a better life
- Save ideas
- Feel inspired
- Emotionally self-identify
- Plan their future

This makes Pinterest **extremely aligned with Reunion** because family connection is deeply aspirational.

---

## What NOT to Do

Do not create boards about:
- App features
- Product updates
- Software / technology

Do not make pins that feel like:
- Obvious ads
- App-looking graphics
- Startup energy

Pinterest users hate branded content. Most content should feel editorial, emotional, save-worthy, and lifestyle-oriented.

## Pin Format Strategy

### 1. Text-Over-Image Emotional Pins (Highest Save Rate)
Examples:
- "One day the loud family dinners become memories."
- "The stories disappear when nobody shares them."
- "The blurry photos become your favorites later."

### 2. Carousel / Step Pins
Examples:
- "10 Questions To Ask Your Grandparents"
- "5 Family Traditions Worth Starting"
- "How To Keep Long-Distance Families Close"

### 3. Cinematic Lifestyle Imagery
Warm: kitchens, couches, old photos, gatherings, grandparents, family tables

### 4. Humor Pins
Family text memes, generational jokes, family chaos observations

## Posting Strategy

- **3–8 pins daily**
- Mostly evergreen content
- Heavy scheduling (use GrowthOS publisher)
- High consistency

Pinterest rewards:
- Consistency
- Keyword richness
- Saves
- Click-throughs

Pinterest does NOT reward viral spikes.
