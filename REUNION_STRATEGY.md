# REUNION — MARKETING STRATEGY REFERENCE
*This document is the canonical marketing strategy for the Reunion brand. All content generation, social publishing, SEO, and AEO work in GrowthOS should align with this doctrine.*

---

## CORE POSITIONING

**Primary Public Tagline:** "A place where generations gather."
**Secondary:** "A shared space for family stories, memories, and connection."
**Internal Framing:** Emotional infrastructure for trusted human connection.

### What Reunion Is NOT (Publicly)
- A tech/AI app
- An "OS" or platform infrastructure tool
- A productivity or utility application
- A feature-first product

### Master Doctrine
> **DO NOT SELL THE APP FIRST. SELL THE PROBLEM FIRST.**

The audience must emotionally agree that:
- Modern families are fragmented
- Stories are disappearing
- Meaningful interaction is declining
- Memories are scattered
- Families want connection but lack systems that create participation

Only after emotional agreement should Reunion be introduced as the natural solution.

## PLATFORM PLAYBOOKS

### INSTAGRAM
- **Role:** Primary emotional brand engine
- **Demo:** 25–45 (primary), 18–24 (secondary)
- **Content Mix:** Reels 50% · Carousels 30% · Stories 15% · Memes 5%
- **Posting:** Reels 1–3/day · Carousels 4–6/week · Stories 5–20/day · Lives 1/week
- **Top Metrics:** SHARES + SAVES (most important)
- **Hook:** Must land in 1.5 seconds
- **Reel length:** 7–35 seconds ideal
- **DO:** Reply to comments fast, pin emotional comments, ask follow-up questions, repost community stories, carousel for saves
- **AVOID:** Repetitive hashtags, engagement bait spam, copied content watermarks, mass follow/unfollow, automation abuse, identical captions

### FACEBOOK
- **Role:** Community + older demographic acquisition
- **Demo:** 35–65+
- **Structure:** Brand Page (authority/ads) + "The Family Connection Project" Group (community/movement)
- **Content:** Emotional videos, relatable memes, long-form posts, discussion prompts, polls, nostalgic images
- **Posting:** Page 2–4/day · Group 5–15 interactions/day
- **Top Metrics:** Shares, comments, active group members, returning commenters
- **DO:** Prioritize group conversations, member-generated stories, welcome new members
- **AVOID:** Political bait, spam links, excessive outbound links, sensationalism, fake urgency

### TIKTOK
- **Role:** Mass awareness + emotional virality
- **Demo:** 18–40 (strongest: 24–38)
- **Posting:** 3–6/day (volume testing)
- **Top Metrics:** Completion rate (most important), average watch time, rewatches, shares, comment velocity
- **DO:** Respond to comments with videos, stitch emotional stories, use trends selectively, prioritize authenticity over polish
- **AVOID:** Recycled low-quality content, overusing hashtags, engagement bait spam, corporate feel

### X (TWITTER)
- **Role:** Thought leadership + cultural positioning
- **Demo:** 25–50 (tech, founders, media, writers)
- **Posting:** 8–20/day
- **Best Formats:** Short insights, threads, provocative observations, relatable truths, statistics
- **Top Metrics:** Impressions, bookmarks, profile clicks, engagement rate, follower growth velocity
- **IMPORTANT:** Reply strategy > posting. Daily: reply to large creators, join trending conversations, quote tweet thoughtfully
- **AVOID:** Rage bait, excessive automation, repetitive posting, copy/paste replies

### THREADS
- **Role:** Conversational intimacy channel + founder personality
- **Demo:** 25–45 (Instagram crossover)
- **Posting:** 5–12/day
- **Tone:** Softer reflections, behind-the-scenes thoughts, emotional observations, relatable questions
- **Use as:** Early audience nurturing, founder authenticity, warm brand voice

### REDDIT
- **Role:** Community intelligence + authority building
- **Demo:** 20–45 (high skepticism)
- **CRITICAL RULE:** DO NOT MARKET LIKE A BRAND. Reddit punishes obvious promotion.
- **Strategy:** Become useful. Participate in: family, parenting, genealogy, nostalgia, loneliness, aging parents, relationships subreddits
- **Build karma slowly:** Thoughtful comments, emotional observations, helpful advice
- **AVOID:** Spam, self-promotion, link dumping, low-effort marketing

### LINKEDIN
- **Role:** Founder authority + business credibility
- **Demo:** 28–55
- **Posting:** 1–2/day
- **Best Formats:** Founder storytelling, essays, carousels, statistics, mission posts
- **Angles:** Loneliness epidemic, modern connection problems, future of trusted communities, emotional technology, startup journey
- **NOT:** Family memes

### PINTEREST
- **Role:** SEO + evergreen discovery (Pinterest is a search engine)
- **Demo:** Women 25–54
- **Posting:** 10–25 pins/day
- **Best Content:** Family traditions, memory ideas, conversation starters, family games, legacy ideas, sentimental quotes
- **SEO Keywords:** "family traditions", "memory preservation", "family connection ideas", "family conversation starters", "family game night"
